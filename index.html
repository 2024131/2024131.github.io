GALACTIC ADVENTURE

An intergalactic journey through space and time. Dodge asteroids, collect power-ups, and save the galaxy!

How to Play

Controls

  • Arrow Keys - Move your spaceship
  • Spacebar - Fire lasers
  • P Key - Pause game

Objectives

  • Destroy enemy ships
  • Collect energy crystals
  • Avoid asteroid fields
  • Reach the final boss

About the Game

Galactic Adventure is a space shooter game created using TurboWarp, a high-performance Scratch mod. This project was developed over 3 months with careful attention to game mechanics and visual design.

Features

Multiple Levels

Progress through 5 challenging space zones with unique enemies and obstacles.

Power-Ups

Collect special items to enhance your spaceship's abilities temporarily.

Boss Battles

Test your skills against massive enemy ships at the end of each zone.

Development Notes

Technologies Used

  • TurboWarp (Scratch mod)
  • Custom sprite designs
  • Original sound effects

Challenges Overcome

The most difficult part was implementing the boss AI patterns while keeping the game performance smooth. Using TurboWarp's optimization features helped achieve consistent frame rates.
